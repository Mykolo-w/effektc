2. Your Data in the App

Everything stays with you (Local Storage)

First and foremost: Your private notes, consumption logs, and effect ratings do not leave your phone. The app stores this sensitive data in a protected database directly on your device. If you delete the app, this data is gone – we have no backup because we have no access to it.

No Account Requirement

You use effekt:C without a user account. We do not ask for your email address or name to use the app. Technically, you are an anonymous user to us.

Statistics for Improvement (Optional)

To understand which cannabis strains have which effect, we collect completely anonymized statistics data.

Example: "Strain XYZ has a relaxing effect on 80% of users."

Important: Your personal texts or private notes are never transmitted. It's purely about the mechanics of the effects.

Permissions

We only ask for permissions that are technically necessary (e.g., storage access so your data can be saved on your phone).

3. Data on this Website

When you visit this website, our server automatically notes standard technical information (e.g., browser, IP address) to keep the site secure and stable. This happens automatically.

4. Your Rights

Since we have no user accounts, you have full control. All your data lies on your device in your hands.
