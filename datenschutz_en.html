2. Your Data in the App

Everything stays with you (Local Storage)

First and foremost: Your private notes, consumption logs, and effect ratings do not leave your phone. The app stores this sensitive data in a protected database directly on your device. If you delete the app, this data is gone – we have no backup because we have no access to it.

No Account Requirement

You use effekt:C without a user account. We do not ask for your email address or name to use the app. Technically, you are an anonymous user to us.

Statistics for Improvement (Optional)

To understand which cannabis strains have which effect, we collect completely anonymized statistics data.

Example: "Strain XYZ has a relaxing effect on 80% of users."

Important: Your personal texts or private notes are never transmitted. It's purely about the mechanics of the effects.

Permissions

We only ask for permissions that are technically necessary (e.g., storage access so your data can be saved on your phone, or notifications for reminders).

Use of Third-Party Providers

To provide and secure the core functions of the app, we work with selected service providers. In doing so, data is transferred to external servers:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google Ireland Limited): We use Firebase Analytics for the statistical evaluation of app usage (e.g., which features are used how often). Technical data such as device type, OS version, and anonymized usage data are transmitted to Google servers (partially in the US). We capture no personal identification features and no sensitive diary contents. The IP address is processed anonymously. The legal basis is our legitimate interest (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR) in analyzing and optimizing the app. A technical opt-out in the app is not currently provided, as these are essential baseline metrics.
  • RevenueCat Inc.: We use the RevenueCat service to process and manage in-app purchases ("Premium Version"). When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes anonymous app user IDs and purchase receipts to validate the unlocking of premium features on your device. No payment information (such as credit card data) is processed by us or RevenueCat (this is done directly via your App Store). The data processing is carried out to fulfill the contract (Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR) and takes place partially on servers in the US.

3. Data on this Website and Contact

Hosting (Vercel)

This website is hosted by the external service provider Vercel Inc. (340 S Lemon Ave #4133, Walnut, CA 91789, USA). When you visit this website, Vercel automatically collects technical data (so-called server log files), including your IP address, date and time of the request, browser type, and operating system. The use of Vercel is based on our legitimate interest in a secure, fast, and efficient provision of our online service in accordance with Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR.

Contact by Email

If you contact us by email (e.g., at info@effektc.com), the data you provide (your email address, possibly your name, and the content of the message) will be stored by us in order to answer your inquiry. We delete the data arising in this context after the storage is no longer necessary, or restrict processing if there are statutory retention requirements. The legal basis for processing this data is Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (if the inquiry is related to the fulfillment of a contract) or Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (our legitimate interest in processing your request).

4. Your Rights

You generally have the rights to information, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability, revocation, and objection regarding your personal data.

Special characteristic in the app: Since we do not have user accounts in the app and cannot identify you, you have full control locally on your device. All your data lies on your smartphone in your hands. You can irretrievably delete them at any time via the app settings.

If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection laws, you can complain to the responsible supervisory authority.
